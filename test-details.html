Test Overview

This comprehensive panel leverages the ultra-sensitive Argo HT platform to simultaneously quantify 250 critical biomarkers associated with neurodegenerative diseases. Validated for both CSF and plasma samples, this assay provides researchers with unprecedented insight into disease mechanisms and progression.

Key Applications:

  • Alzheimer's disease biomarker discovery and validation
  • Parkinson's disease progression monitoring
  • ALS and FTD research applications
  • Multi-target drug development studies
  • Population health screening initiatives

Clinical Relevance:

This panel includes FDA-breakthrough designated biomarkers (p-tau217, NfL) alongside emerging targets validated in recent clinical trials. The comprehensive coverage enables hypothesis-free discovery while ensuring coverage of established clinical markers.

Protocols & Standard Operating Procedures

Sample Collection Protocol:

  1. Collect blood samples in EDTA or heparin tubes (preferred: EDTA)
  2. Process within 2 hours of collection for optimal results
  3. Centrifuge at 2000g for 10 minutes at 4¬∞C
  4. Aliquot plasma into cryovials (minimum 50ŒºL aliquots)
  5. Flash freeze and store at -80¬∞C
  6. Ship on dry ice with temperature monitoring

Quality Control:

  • All samples run in duplicate
  • Calibration curve with each batch (8-point curve)
  • High, medium, and low QC samples included
  • Spike recovery validation for each batch
  • Acceptance criteria: CV < 15% for all QCs

Data Delivery:

  • Results delivered via secure NAS portal
  • Raw data (MFI values) included
  • Calibration curves and QC data provided
  • Statistical analysis summary report
  • Certificate of analysis for each batch
